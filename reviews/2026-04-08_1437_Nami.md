## 3 Things to Work On

**1. Nocturne-aware positioning — stay behind your ADC when his R is available**
In this game (E) Nocturne killed you or contributed to your death at 07:30, 15:08, and 28:46. The pattern: you were exposed and forward when he R'd. Nocturne's R makes him invisible and lets him dive the softest target instantly — that's always the support. Track his R cooldown (roughly every 90 seconds after level 6) and during that window stand strictly behind (A) Samira, not beside her. The moment the screen goes dark, step backward into your team and hold Oracle Lens ready.

**2. Post-fight discipline — stop moving after a fight ends**
Three of your six deaths came within 30 seconds of a fight finishing (15:08, 16:25, 17:45). The pattern: your team got a kill or took damage, and instead of resetting position everyone kept moving forward — and you died in the chaos. After every fight, stay within 600 units of your nearest ally for 15 seconds before going anywhere. Call retreat in chat if needed. One extra heal from safety is worth more than following someone forward into a death.

**3. Use Tidal Wave in every teamfight and every major objective contest**
You applied 29 seconds of CC across a 29-minute game — roughly 1 second per minute. Nami's Q (1.5s bubble) and Tidal Wave alone should produce 3–5x that. In the 17:25 wipe, a Tidal Wave through mid lane at the moment (E) Yone engaged would have knocked up the entire blue team frontline. In the uncontested Baron at 22:36, a Tidal Wave through the pit entrance forces them to scatter and buys 3–5 seconds. Cast your ult in every fight. If a fight ends and you're not sure if you ulted, you held it too long.
