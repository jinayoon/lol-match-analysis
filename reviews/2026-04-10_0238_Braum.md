## 3 Things to Work On (detailed)

**1. Post-kill positioning**
Deaths at 6:49, 13:33, 17:28, and 35:45 all followed winning exchanges. The pattern: your team wins a skirmish, pressure feels safe, your team lingers, then the enemy jungler or support arrives. After every kill, physically move toward safety — tower, warded area, lane brush — for 10-15 seconds before deciding whether to extend. You don't need to recall every time; just close the distance to a defensible position. This habit alone eliminates 3-4 of your 8 deaths.

**2. Knight's Vow timing**
Lucian was your primary carry from the first minute. Knight's Vow (12% damage redirect + bonus resistances for the bonded ally) wasn't completed until 25:34 — after the 13:33 catch on Lucian, the 17:24 dragon pit death, and the 22:39 solo kill on him. Identify your carry in the first 5 minutes and commit: Bandlepipes first (you had it by 11:32), Knight's Vow second (target 16-18 minutes). Active protection earlier directly prevents the carry deaths that let the enemy team back into the game.

**3. Vision before objectives**
Two of your deaths — 17:28 at dragon pit and 25:04 at Baron pit — followed the same pattern: walking into a contested objective area with Blitzcrank unaccounted for and no control ward in the pit. As the support, you own this setup. Before your team commits to dragon or Baron, drop a control ward inside the pit and check both river entrances. If Blitzcrank isn't on the minimap, call your team to wait 10 seconds until he appears. The cost of one control ward and 10 seconds of patience versus the cost of a failed objective fight isn't close.
