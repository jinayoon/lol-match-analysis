## 3 Things to Work On

**1. Plant-range discipline in teamfights — never step inside your seed's root distance**
In the 11:27 fight in the mid lane, you died 6 seconds after (A) Briar, meaning you were at frontline distance when (E) Ryze pivoted onto you. (A) Zyra's power is entirely range-dependent — her seeds plant at 800 units and create a zone that enemies must walk through to reach you. If you can auto-attack the enemy, you're too close. When a fight starts, ask yourself: "Am I at max range?" If not, step backward before casting anything. A seed planted from the correct distance does the same CC while keeping you out of burst range.

**2. Account for (E) Gnar (and any split-pushing bruiser) before any lateral movement**
At 23:35, (E) Gnar caught you alone in mid lane near your inner turret with zero assist — a pure positional mistake. He was 7/4 on a champion that one-shots squishy backlines in mini form. The fix takes 0.5 seconds: before stepping into any brush, flanking around a corner, or moving laterally in the mid-to-late game, glance at the minimap and find the enemy bruiser's icon. If it's missing from where it should be, don't move until you see it.

**3. Complete Grievous Wounds — don't hold Oblivion Orb for more than 6 minutes**
You bought Oblivion Orb at 23:42 and never completed it into Morellonomicon, holding it as a dead 800-gold investment for 14 minutes. Full Morellonomicon applies 40% Grievous Wounds on ability hit — directly reducing (E) Rakan's shields on his allies and (E) Ryze's late-game durability. Every time (A) Zyra's plants hit an enemy in those 14 minutes, they could have been applying Grievous Wounds and weren't. Set a rule: if you buy Oblivion Orb, complete Morellonomicon within 6 minutes or sell it and build something you'll actually finish.
